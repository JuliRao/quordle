Quordle for Swifties!

In Quordle Wordle game, you have to guess 4 words in 10 tries. After each guess, the color of the letters will change to give you clues about the hidden word.
The colors indicate if letters are correct: green for right place, yellow for wrong place, and gray for not in the word.
